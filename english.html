THE PURPOSE OF
24 DAYS OF CULTURE

24 Days With Culture is an initiative launched by the KulturSkaber office in Kultur N. The purpose of this December cultural festival is to showcase a selection of the many colorful cultural entrepreneurships in Nørrebro. With 24 Days of Culture we put our finger on Nørrebro's pulse and make this visible to Nørrebro and the rest of the city.

The event is a recurring event/happening every December, if you want to attend next time you can contact the KulturSkaberne. For already involved hosts, we have put together a list with tips to make your event even better.
